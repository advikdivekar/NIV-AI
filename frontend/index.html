How Niv AI Works

Niv AI runs your home-buying decision through 6 specialized AI agents, each focused on a different dimension of the decision.

📋 Agent 1: Context Synthesizer

Structures your financial and property data. Identifies implicit assumptions. Flags missing information.

💰 Agent 2: Financial Analyst

Evaluates true affordability — not just EMI, but total cost of ownership, cash flow impact, and tax benefits.

⚠️ Agent 3: Risk Simulator

Stress-tests your decision against 4 real scenarios: job loss, interest rate hikes, unexpected expenses, and income stagnation.

🏠 Agent 4: Property Analyst

Evaluates the property against Mumbai market benchmarks. Checks pricing fairness, location risks, and rent-vs-buy math.

🔍 Agent 5: Assumption Challenger

The adversarial agent — finds holes in your assumptions, identifies blind spots, and flags emotional bias in your decision.

⚖️ Agent 6: Decision Composer

Synthesizes all analyses into a clear verdict (Safe / Risky / Reconsider) with specific reasoning and actionable advice.

Trust & Transparency

Every financial number in the report is calculated by deterministic code — not generated by AI. The AI interprets and explains, but never invents numbers. All reasoning is traceable.
